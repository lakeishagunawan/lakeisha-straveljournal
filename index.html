Lakeisha's Travel Journal

Exploring the world, one step at a time

Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo is a vibrant city full of modern skyscrapers, crowded streets and amazing culture. I explored Shibuya Crossing, visited Akusa Temple with my family, and enjoyed the lively city vibes.

Bali, Indonesia

Bali is a tropical paradise with beautiful beaches and rich culture. I really enjoy looking at the sunsets there and ate delicious local food. The acivities like swimming with dolphin, rafting and riding ATV was really fun.

Singapore

Singapore is a modern city with beautiful gardens and impressive skyscrapers. I visited Gardens by the Bay, tried local street foods and went to Universal Studios Singapore.

Gili Trawangan, Indonesia

Gili Trawangan is a small island with clear blue waters and a relaxing atmosphere. I went snorkeling, enjoyed sunset, and rode a bicylce around the peaceful island.
